Future Placement has the areas of specialization which is divided into few categories but not limited to.

Hospitality
The main three bifurcation of hospitality industry goes with Hotel Industry, Commercial Catering Services and Restaurant Chain for fine dining and QSR both.
Again we are having an enriched DATA bank of all the four divisions of hospitality industry like Production, Housekeeping, F&B Services and Front Office.
We are assembled with an expertise Hospitality Recruitment Team and having a pipeline of candidates who are qualified with Degrees and Diploma in Hospitality Management.
Oil and Gas
The biggest industry of GCC countries is Oil & Gas, we have learned from our experience and expertise to source for both long term and shut down process. To deliver required volume of manpower within defined timeframe is the main criteria of this industry.
We at Future Placement, keep a pipeline of skilled people for the industry like different scale of Engineers, HSE Engineers, Rigger, Different Types of Welder, Fitter, Gas Cutter, Electrician – High & Low Voltage, Fabricator, Pipe Fitter and other required skill sets.
Construction
Construction and Real Estate is the most fast moving industry type in GCC countries. We have Civil Engineers with different rack of experience like Project Management, Quality Control and Billing etc. To start up with Blue Collar stuffing we have a huge pool of DATA base for Civil Category - Shuttering Carpenter, Steel Fixer, Marble Mason, Block Mason, Tiles Mason, Scaffolder, Painter, and Gypsum Carpenter, Painter and False ceiling.
MEP Category – Electrician, Plumber, Duct Fabricator & Fitter, Sheet Metal Fabricator, Insulator, HVAC Tech and more.
Facility
FM business plays an important role to maintain the country’s infrastructure with proper hygiene and life style measures.
Future Placement is into both Hard and Soft Facilities Management (FM) services, keeping a huge DATA base of people with different skill set like, Cleaners Security Guards, HVAC Technician Split and Window AC Technician, Plumber, Electrician, Diesel Generator Operator and Technician, Cleaner, Gardener and more.
Textile
The textile industry in GCC is estimated to grow at a CAGR of ~3.8% over the forecast period of 2019-2024. A huge number of work force is required to fill the gap which is in abundance in the countries like India, Nepal and Bangladesh.
Healthcare
A high dependence on expatriates and inherent scarcity of skilled and experienced Doctors, Nurses, Lab Technicians and Home Care has been one of the major factors hindering the growth of the healthcare sector in the GCC. On average, while the physicians (including dentists) to population ratio are close to that in the other developed countries, the region faces a dearth of nursing staff. With new hospital developments underway, the competition to hire experienced and skilled physicians, nurses and allied workforce is set to intensify. To meet the growing need of qualified healthcare professionals, Future Placement has established a huge number of Healthcare Service Providers’ database to support.
