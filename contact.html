Globe Consultant is a manpower recruitment agency mainly focused in the Gulf Countries. We started in the year 2014 as one of the pioneer recruitment agency for Gulf placement in eastern India. Our professional team looks in to the requirement of overseas placement of skilled and non-skilled staff for hospitality, healthcare, general labour in recruitment’s to GCC Countries and around the world.
Today Globe Consultant is a renowned name in the Manpower Industry, who ranks among the leading Overseas Recruitment companies in India. Located in Kolkata, we have built a reputation in the market with our efficiency in “placing the right candidate for the right job”.
The world looks at India as the one great source of world class skills in the fields of Medicine, Health Care, Engineering, Computer Science and many more. Globe Consultant, is dedicated to offer the specialized services to the core areas to our diverse clients.
We are a licensed  Placement Agency approved by the Government of India – issued by the ‘Ministry of External Affairs’.
